Our Story

Howard's Lakeside General Store has been a cornerstone of the Springfield, Ohio community since 1975. Founded by Howard himself, the store started as a small bait shop catering to local fishermen. Over the years, we have expanded our offerings to include a wide range of products such as live bait, tackle, fishing and hunting licenses, camping supplies, firewood, drinks, and snacks.

We pride ourselves on being a family-owned and operated business that values quality and customer satisfaction above all. Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to assist you with finding the perfect gear for your outdoor adventures, whether you're a seasoned angler or a weekend camper.

At Howard's Lakeside General Store, we believe in giving back to our community. We regularly host fishing tournaments, sponsor local events, and support conservation efforts to ensure that future generations can enjoy the natural beauty of our local lakes and rivers. Our store has become a gathering place for outdoor enthusiasts of all ages, a place where stories are shared, and memories are made.

Our mission is simple: to provide our customers with high-quality products and exceptional service. We are committed to maintaining a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels like part of the family. Thank you for choosing Howard's Lakeside General Store for your outdoor needs. We look forward to serving you for many more years to come.
